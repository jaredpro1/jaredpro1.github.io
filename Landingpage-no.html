JESUS CHRIST

John 3:16 :- For GOD so loved the world, that he gave his only begotten SON , that whosoever believeth in him should not perish, but have everlasting life. Yes JESUS CHRIST , the SON of GOD who came on down form heaven and offered himself for us and brutally died for our sins, would you give your child or if you do not have one your brother or sister or friend to die to save a guilty person. Well GOD did that, and you are saved by faith, Ephesians 2 verses 8-9 :- 8 For by grace are ye saved through faith; and that not of yourselves: it is the gift of GOD: 9 Not of works, lest any man should boast. Also Luke Chapter 13 verse 3 :- I tell you, Nay: but, except ye repent, ye shall; all likewise perish. Although we are saved by faith is there also repentance from sin, is sin, if you do not get saved from it are you going to eternal punishment. Can someone say the sinners prayer or say they have faith or go to a church every single week but if you do not repent form sin are you going to eternal punishment. Romans chapter 8 verse 13:- For if ye live after the flesh, ye shall die: but if ye through the Spirit do mortify the deeds of the body, ye shall live. Faith yes but is there also repentance.
