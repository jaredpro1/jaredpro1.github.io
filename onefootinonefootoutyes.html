JESUS CHRIST

Psalm 51 verse 16 -17 :- For thou desirest not sacrifice else; else would I give it: thou delightest not in burnt offering. 17 The sacrifices of GOD are a broken spirit: a broken and a contrite heart, O GOD, thou wilt not despise.
